Think of PropSift as your **virtual assistant** that organizes your leads, tells you who to call, and makes sure no opportunity slips through the cracks.

### 2. Smart Scoring

PropSift automatically calculates a **priority score** for each lead based on:

| Factor | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Motivations** | Why they might sell (divorce, probate, tax liens, etc.) |
| **Data Quality** | How complete and accurate the information is |
| **Engagement** | Have they responded to previous contact? |
| **Recency** | When was the lead added or last contacted? |
| **Phone Status** | Do we have good phone numbers? |

**Score Ranges:**
- **90-100** ‚Üí Call Now (highest priority)
- **50-89** ‚Üí Call Queue (regular priority)
- **Below 50** ‚Üí Nurture (lower priority)

This means you always know which leads deserve your attention first.

### 5. Lead Cadence Engine (LCE)

The brain behind PropSift's follow-up system. The LCE:

- **Schedules follow-ups** automatically based on temperature
- **Tracks call attempts** and advances through the cadence
- **Hides called leads** until their next scheduled date
- **Adjusts priority** based on call results and status changes

**How it works:**
1. You call a lead and log the result
2. LCE calculates when to call next
3. Lead disappears from your queue
4. Lead reappears on the scheduled date
5. Repeat until deal closes or lead exits

This ensures **persistent, systematic follow-up** without manual tracking.

## How PropSift Works (The Big Picture)

```
Import Leads ‚Üí Score & Prioritize ‚Üí Work in DockInsight ‚Üí Log Results ‚Üí Auto-Schedule ‚Üí Repeat
```

### Step 1: Import Your Leads
Upload your lead lists or add leads manually. PropSift organizes them automatically.

### Step 2: Leads Get Scored
The system analyzes each lead and assigns a priority score and temperature.

### Step 3: Work in DockInsight
Open DockInsight to see your prioritized queue. Start with "Call Now" leads.

### Step 4: Make Calls & Log Results
Call each lead, then log what happened (answered, no answer, wrong number, etc.)

### Step 5: System Schedules Follow-Up
Based on the result, PropSift schedules the next call and hides the lead until then.

### Step 6: Leads Reappear When Due
On the scheduled date, leads pop back into your queue ready to call again.

### Step 7: Repeat Until Closed
Continue the cycle until the lead becomes a deal, goes DNC, or is marked dead.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I import leads?
Go to Records ‚Üí Import and upload a CSV file with your lead data.

### Q: How do I add a new lead manually?
Go to Records ‚Üí Add Record and fill in the property and owner details.

### Q: How do I change a lead's temperature?
Update their status to "Interested" (upgrades) or "Not Interested" (downgrades).

### Q: How do I remove a lead from my queue?
Set their status to DNC, Sold, or Dead.

### Q: How do I see my team's activity?
Check the Stats sidebar in DockInsight or go to the Activity page.

### Q: Can I customize the statuses and call results?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Statuses or Settings ‚Üí Call Results to create your own.

### Q: What happens if I don't call for a few days?
Leads stay in your queue (they become overdue). When you return, they'll be waiting.

### Q: How do I contact support?
Email support@propsift.com or use the help chat in the app.
